About Us

Every year 3,442 to 4,262 stray animals get injured in road accidents and 1,200 human injuries occur at the same time.

On 24th July 2021 two of the cyclist buddies (Hitesh Kuniyal and Rohan Deshwal) took the initiative of putting up the reflective collars on stray animals that can go a long way in reducing the animal road accidents.

Reflecting collars function the same way a road stud does. They work as regular collars for animals but what sets them apart is the reflective strip that shines brightly when a vehicle's headlight falls on it. This alerts the driver to the animal's presence in low light conditions and reduces the risk of an accident.
